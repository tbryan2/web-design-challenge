City Latitude vs. Maximum Temperature

This is a plot of the cities' latitude vs. their maximum temperature. There's a clear correlation between distance from the equator and the temperature. The relationship is asymetrical skewing towards the north; in other words, the drop off of temperature as you get farther from the equator going north is greater than the drop off of temperature as you get farther from the equator going south.







The x-axis displays the latitude of the city. The y-axis displays the maximum temperature (in F) of the city.
